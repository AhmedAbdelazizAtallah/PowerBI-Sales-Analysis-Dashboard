# 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Sales Analysis Dashboard** developed using **Microsoft Power BI** to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights.

The dashboard enables decision-makers to monitor key business metrics, analyze sales performance, evaluate profitability, compare regional performance, identify customer purchasing behavior, and investigate profit drivers using interactive visualizations.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Organizations collect large volumes of sales data every day. Without proper analysis, it becomes difficult to identify performance trends, evaluate profitability, and make informed business decisions.

This dashboard was designed to solve these challenges by providing an interactive Business Intelligence solution that helps stakeholders understand sales performance from multiple perspectives.

---

# 🎯 Project Objectives

- Monitor overall business performance
- Analyze yearly sales trends
- Track profitability across different regions
- Compare product categories and sub-categories
- Evaluate customer purchasing behavior
- Identify factors affecting profit
- Support strategic business decision-making

# 📊 Business Insights

### Executive Performance

- Total Sales reached **33.95 Million**.
- Total Profit reached **6.71 Million**.
- Nearly **10,000 customer orders** were processed.

---

### Sales Trends

- 2017 achieved the highest annual sales.
- 2015 recorded the lowest annual sales.
- Sales and Order Count show a strong positive relationship.

---

### Regional Insights

- West generated the highest profit.
- East ranked second.
- Central produced comparatively lower profitability.

---

### Product Insights

Top-performing Sub-Categories include:

- Phones
- Chairs
- Machines
- Copiers
- Tables

Technology products are the strongest revenue contributors.

---

### Payment Insights

Sales are almost equally distributed between:

- Cash
- Credit Card

This balanced distribution reduces dependency on one payment method.

---

### Category Insights

Technology delivers the highest profitability.

Furniture generates high sales but lower profit margins.

Office Supplies maintains stable sales while offering opportunities for margin improvement.

---

### Root Cause Analysis

Key Influencers analysis indicates that Office Supplies contributes significantly to lower profitability under certain business conditions.

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

- Increase investment in Technology products.
- Review pricing strategy for Furniture products.
- Improve profitability of Office Supplies.
- Focus marketing campaigns on high-performing regions.
- Investigate low-performing regions.
- Expand inventory for best-selling products.
- Continue monitoring KPIs using interactive dashboards.
- Use predictive analytics for future sales forecasting.
